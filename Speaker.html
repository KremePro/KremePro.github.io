We're happy to welcome over twenty speaker to present on the industry's latest technologies.

Prepare for an inspiration extravaganze.

Aaron Irizarry

Workplace culture doesn't start with beanbags, foosball tables, or a beer fridge and it doesn't end with neckties, Pcs, or big corporations, It's the unwritten rules, behavior, beliefs, and the motivations that enable good work to get done, or it's what stifles a workforce. For design to be most effective an for designers to fell valued, we need to work in culture that embraces design and allows it to succeed.

Adam Connor

Adam Connor is a designer, strategist, author and illustrator based in Western Massachusetts. Adam's work focuses on helping teams and organizations build their own human-centered design and innovation capabilities.

AJ Self

AJ is a software engineer specializing in Javascript working at Belly in Chicago. Lately he has been writing application with AngularJS and loving it. When not coding he is out loving the outdoor with his dog, Sunshine.

Arman Ghosh

Arman was the Managing Partner & Co-Founder of A-Frame Ventures (acquired) where his team built a portfolio of companies including Aptitude 8 & GreatVet. Additionally A-Frame partnered with companies like Gordon Food Services, Bosch, UpCity and Ginger.io to help build world class businesses.

Bermon Painter

Bermon has more than 20 years of product strategy, design, and development experience in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, financial services, government, manufacturing, publishing, and retail.

Shay Howe

Shay is the top product leader in Chicago. Hands down. When not speaking at the best conferences, writing books, or having a beer at Jake's - he is leading people through processes that he studied and executed to design products from ideation to deployment.
